Édémédé egosipụtara

Dora Nkem Akunyili (amụrụ ya na abali 14 n'ọnwa asaa n-afọ 1954 – ya anwụọ n'abali asaa n'ọnwa isii na-afọ 2014). Ọ bụ onye Ntụziaka nke Ụlọ-ọru ndi (Director General) National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control (NAFDAC) nile di na Naijiria. Bụkwara Onye isi Mgbasaozi nke ala Naijiria (Nigerian Minister of Information and Communications) site na-afọ 2008 ruo 2010.

Ọ bụ onye Ntọ Ọgwụ nke ndi bekee na-akpọ Pharmacist nakwa onye Ọrụ Gọvament ama-ama na mba ụwa tinyekwara n'onwetara ọtụtụ inwe site n'ọrụ ya 'Pharmacology', 'Public Health' na 'Human Rights
Hụ édémédé ndị ọzọ egosipụtara

Foto egosipụtara

Ọjà bụ ngwa egwu si n'ọwụwa anyanwụ Naijiria nke a na-ejikarị ngwa ndị Naijiria ndị ọzọ dị ka ekwe, udu, igba, okwa na ichaka/osha eji agu egwu. Ngwá egwú a pụrụ iche ‘na-ekwu okwu’ ka ndị na-akụ egwu na ndị na-agba egwú, na-agba egwu. N'oge a na-agba egwu mmawu na ọwụwa anyanwụ Naijiria, onye ọfụfụ oja na-edu egwu na abụ otuto. Onye isi ofufu ọjà na-enwe ike iwepụta ọtụtụ ụda dị ka okwu ọnụ. Ọ bụrụ na onye dị mkpa abanye n'ime oghere igba egwu, onye n'afu ọjà nwere ike iji ngwá egwu a kpọsaa aha onye ahụ.

Ị mà na...
  • Igbo hailaif malitere mbụ na mpaghara ndịda ọwụwa anyanwụ nke Naịjirịa, n'oge 1950
  • Ikwokirikwo (Ikwokilikwo) bụ ụdị egwu Igbo dị elu nke na-emetụta Ogene, Ụdị a pụtara na mbido 1970s.
  • Egwu Atilogwu so na egwu ọdịnala a na-agba na Enugu Steeti
  • Bongo Owerri (Bongo/Bongo Igbo) bụ ụdị egwu Igbo dị elu sitere na ndị Igbo bi na Owerri.
  • Odumodu bụ ụdị egwu ndị mmadụ si Arochukwu, Bende, Ohafia, Abiriba, Umuahia, Ikwuano, na Ngwa ndị Igbo, nke Abia steeti.
  • Egwu Egedege bụ otu n'ime ekike egwu ọdịnala ama ama na egwu ọdịnala ndị eze nke Igbo na-eme na Anambra.
  • Egwu Agbachaekurunwa bụ ihe eji mara ndi Ụmụcho Ụmụediabali Ihitteaforukwu Ahịa azụ Mbaise n'Imo Steeti.
  • Agborogwụ ma ọ bụ Ekereavụ bụ egwu eji mara Ogorinawuibe, Umumbiri Oparanadim Ekwerazu, Ahịa azụ Mbaise n'Imo steeti
  • Egwu MMỤTAKA bụ egwu ndi obodo Ubaha nke di n' Umunze Anambra Steeti. Egwu a si n' egwu a na-akpo Mkpọkịtị wee pụta
  • Ndị ọzọ bụ rap Igbo, Ekpili, Ogene, gospel Igbo, Ikorodo, wdg
