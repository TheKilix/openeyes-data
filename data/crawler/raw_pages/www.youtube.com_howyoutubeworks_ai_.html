How AI-powered Content Moderation works

YouTube has always used a combination of people and machine learning technologies to enforce our Community Guidelines. In our systems, AI classifiers help detect potentially violative content at scale, and reviewers work to confirm whether content has actually crossed policy lines. AI is continuously increasing both the speed and accuracy of our content moderation systems.

We also work with other companies across the technology and media industries to establish commitments and standards that support broader responsibility around Gen AI, including the Tech Accord on Deceptive AI Election Content, and C2PA .
